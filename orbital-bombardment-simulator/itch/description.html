🌎 Orbital Bombardment: Planetary Destruction Simulator 🚀

Embark on an interstellar journey of destruction in this addictive idle game! 🌠

🎮 Game Overview

In Orbital Bombardment, you command a fleet of powerful spaceships tasked with destroying planets across the galaxy. Start small and work your way up to become the ultimate planet-destroying force in the universe!

✨ Key Features

  • 🛸 Multiple ship types with unique abilities
  • 🌍 Diverse planets to destroy, each with its own challenges
  • 💥 Upgrade your fleet for maximum destruction
  • 🏆 Prestige system for long-term progression
  • 🎨 Beautiful space-themed graphics

🕹️ How to Play

  1. 🚀 Start with a small fleet of ships orbiting a planet
  2. 💰 Earn money by damaging the planet
  3. 🛒 Buy more ships to increase your damage output
  4. 🔧 Upgrade your ships for better performance
  5. 🌠 Destroy planets to progress through the solar system
  6. 🔄 Prestige to unlock powerful bonuses and start anew

🚢 Ship Types

Ship Type Description
Small Ships Fast and agile, perfect for beginners
Medium Ships Balanced firepower and speed
Large Ships Slow but incredibly powerful

🆙 Upgrades

Enhance your fleet's performance with various upgrades:

Upgrade Effect
Cooldown reduction Increase fire rate
Damage boost Deal more damage per shot

🌟 Prestige System

Once you've destroyed enough planets, you can prestige to earn gold and unlock permanent bonuses:

Bonus Effect
Money multiplier Earn more money from planet destruction
Damage multiplier Increase overall fleet damage

💡 Tips for Success

  • Balance your fleet composition for maximum efficiency
  • Upgrade your ships regularly to keep up with tougher planets
  • Don't forget to prestige when progress slows down
  • Experiment with different strategies to find your optimal playstyle
